Formania – A Strategic Puzzle Game for Android

Formania is a free, competitive Android board game. Match shapes or colors, plan your moves, and score big – whether you play solo, with friends, or against smart AI opponents.

🧠 Features

  • 🧠 Strategic gameplay with shape and color combinations
  • 🎮 Up to 4 players – play against friends or AI
  • 🤖 Adjustable AI difficulty: Easy, Medium, Hard
  • 🎯 Play with 5 or 6 symbols per row
  • 🏆 Set your own point goal: 50, 75, or 100
  • 📱 Offline play, no account needed
  • 📖 Optional tutorial for new players
